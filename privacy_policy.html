Introduction

ZohoSyncer is a powerful plugin designed to synchronize leads, contacts, accounts, calls, meetings, reports, and other essential data between multiple Zoho accounts in real-time. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and your rights regarding it.

Data Collection

ZohoSyncer collects the minimum amount of data required to provide its features, including:

  • Zoho account credentials for synchronization purposes.
  • Data related to leads, contacts, accounts, calls, meetings, and reports.
  • Settings and preferences for account connections and sync behavior.

Data Usage

The data collected by ZohoSyncer is used exclusively for providing the following services:

  • Real-time data synchronization between connected Zoho accounts.
  • Manual synchronization with deduplication to prevent data redundancy.
  • Account and lead management, including email functionality.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and implement industry-standard measures to protect it, including:

  • Encrypted storage and transfer of sensitive information.
  • Authentication mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Regular security audits and updates.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request access to your data stored by ZohoSyncer.
  • Correction: Request corrections to any inaccuracies in your data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your data from our system.

Third-Party Sharing

ZohoSyncer does not share your data with third parties, except as required by law or to provide the service (e.g., integration with Zoho's API). Your data remains confidential and secure.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. You will be notified of significant changes through email or an in-app notification.
